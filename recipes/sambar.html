sambar

Sambar is a flavorful and tangy South Indian lentil soup, typically made with toor dal (pigeon peas) and a variety of vegetables. It is seasoned with spices like mustard seeds, cumin, and curry leaves, giving it a distinct taste. Sambar is a staple dish served with rice, idli, dosa, or vada, and is loved for its nutritious ingredients and comforting flavors.

  • 1 cup toor dal (pigeon peas)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind pulp
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
